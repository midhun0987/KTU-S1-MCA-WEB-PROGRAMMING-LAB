About Us

Welcome to Muthoot Institute of Technology & Science (MITS), a premier institution in the country, promoted by Muthoot M. George Institute of Technology , a non-profit trust. Our institution is committed to providing quality education, fostering innovation, and developing socially responsible citizens.

At MITS, we believe in empowering our students with the knowledge, skills, and values required to succeed in an increasingly complex and competitive world. Our faculty, comprising experienced professionals and renowned academicians, are dedicated to providing personalized attention and guidance to each student.
